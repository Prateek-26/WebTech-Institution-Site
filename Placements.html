About T & P

Our Team

The Goa College of Engineering is well known for its academic excellence and has always been very proactive in placing our undergraduate and postgraduate students into the profiles of their interest. The institute has a well-organized placement team and centrally handles all aspects of placements at GEC, right from contacting companies to managing all logistics of arranging for tests, pre-placement talks and conducting the interviews. The Cell also organizes expert lectures, workshops and seminars on technical, aptitude and interview handling skills along with continuous career guidance, The Placement Cell has maintained a prolific placement record over the years in Goa and has become a sought after academic institution.
